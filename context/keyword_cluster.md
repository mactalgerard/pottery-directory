## Competitive Intel

### Main Threats
- **ClassBento** (classbento.com) — paid booking marketplace with city-level pages (NYC, LA, SF, Chicago, etc.). Different model (marketplace vs. free directory) but competes for city-level SERPs. Strongest in major metros — target mid-tier cities (100k–500k population) first where ClassBento is weakest.
- **KilnFire.com** — thin existing directory, city landing page stubs, low authority (rank 53 in competitor list). Beatable on content depth and enrichment.

### Non-Threats (Individual Studios)
All other top SERP results are individual studio websites — not directories. No dominant national directory exists for this niche. The SERP is wide open for a well-built aggregator.

## Monetization Strategy
- **Primary:** Display ads (Google AdSense → Mediavine at 50k sessions/month). SV justifies this at scale.
- **Secondary:** Lead generation / featured studio listings once traffic hits 10k+/month. Studios charge $35–$200/class, so per-lead value is real.
- **Not viable yet:** Sponsored listings (CPC too low at $1.00 to signal strong studio ad spend)
- **Weak:** Affiliate (pottery supplies are low-ticket)
