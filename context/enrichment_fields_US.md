## Research Summary

Research across Reddit (r/Pottery, r/Ceramics), Ceramic Arts Daily community forums, studio websites, Yelp/Google Maps listings, and pottery booking platforms reveals that US pottery studio searchers prioritize: (1) class types and format, (2) whether a studio is beginner-friendly, (3) availability of open studio / membership access, (4) BYOB/social events, and (5) private event hosting. The distinction between structured classes, open studio memberships, and one-time workshops is the single most important differentiator in the US market. BYOB "Sip & Spin" events are a uniquely prominent US format that warrants its own field, and firing-only services emerged as a critical offering for home potters.
